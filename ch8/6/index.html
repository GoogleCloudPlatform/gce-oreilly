Perfuse is an example application from Chapter 8 of "Google Compute Engine" from O'Reilly Media, by Marc Cohen, Kathryn Hurley, and Paul Newson. Perfuse distributes performance test requests to a cluster of servers and summarizes the results graphically and dynamically, as they stream in from the test machines.
